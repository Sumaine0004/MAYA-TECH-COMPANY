About Unity SACCO

Unity SACCO is a community-based cooperative organization focused on improving financial stability and inclusion for our members. We provide access to affordable loans, secure savings, and financial literacy programs.

Founded in 2015, we have helped hundreds of families and entrepreneurs achieve their financial goals through trust, transparency, and teamwork.
